Cryptomanager

Group of eighth graders facilitated by me created an app called Cryptomanager, that centralizes all cryptocurrencies, and creates a custom “homepage” so the user can easily manage, buy, and sell their different cryptocurrencies. Users can talk to professional businessmen who provide expert tips on how, where, why, and when to invest their cryptocurrencies. It also provides a comprehensive social platform in which the user can communicate with their buyer/seller.

*Tech Stack: Python, CSS, JS.

Rights and Resources

The app created, Rights and Resources, answers two questions: “What are my rights in a specific situation?” and “What resources are available to me regarding this situation?” The app incorporates surrounding resources available and simplifies laws. The app is organized by topic and need: a user selects a button from the main screen such as “police” or “mental health” which takes them to another page in order to select a page on “rights” or ”help a friend”.

*Tech Stack: Android Studios, Javascript, CSS, HTML, JSON.
